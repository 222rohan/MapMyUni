University Library

The central library offers five floors of study spaces, research resources, and meeting rooms. Open 24/7 during exam periods.

Student Comments (2)

Dining

Student Center Café

Popular meeting spot with a variety of food options, coffee, and comfortable seating. Offers student discounts and weekly specials.

Student Comments (3)

Recreation

Recreation Center

State-of-the-art fitness facilities including gym, pool, basketball courts, and various fitness classes. Free for students with ID.

Student Comments (1)

Casey M. 2 days ago
